About Us

Developers Connect Philippines (DevCon) is a volunteer organization that aims to promote collaborative growth and competence of developers in the Philippines.

DEVCON SUMMIT 2017: CDO EDITION

In this satellite edition, DevCon brings the Biggest Developer Conference in the Philippines to the progressing golden city of Cagayan de Oro.

Why join DevCon Summit: CDO Edition?

  • Technology Takeaways
    Learn the latest trends and tools, best practices in the IT industry
  • Career Growth
    Stay updated on the IT career opportunities in CDO and Northern Mindanao
  • Collaboration Opportunity
    Connect with our featured speakers from the international, national and local tech industry

All these in an effort to promote the growth and competence of Kagay-anon developers!

Be updated with raffles and pre-event announcements!
